About Me

Hi! I'm a passionate web developer and tech educator dedicated to crafting beautiful, responsive web applications. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and explaining complex concepts in simple terms. Whether building from scratch or optimizing existing projects, I focus on delivering solutions that are both functional and user-friendly.

My Vision

My vision is to empower young people like me to see technology not as something distant or intimidating, but as a tool they can pick up and shape their own future with. I want to help close the gap between curiosity and capability — turning "I wish I knew how to build that" into "I built that."

Growing up figuring this out mostly on my own taught me that the right explanation at the right time changes everything. That's why I tutor as seriously as I build: every young person deserves someone who will break down the confusing parts, answer the "obvious" questions without judgment, and show them that a career in tech is genuinely within reach.

How I Work

I keep things simple: understand what you're trying to achieve, explain the "why" behind every concept (not just the "how"), and build or teach in small, confident steps rather than overwhelming jargon. Whether I'm writing code for a client or sitting down with a student, the goal is the same — leave you with something that works and an understanding of how it works.
